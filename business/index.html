About us

Our Mission

At CapitalTrust Loans, we believe in empowering businesses and individuals with the financial resources they need to turn ambitions into achievements. Our mission is simple: to provide accessible, transparent, and flexible lending solutions that support your goals and foster your growth.

Our Vision

To become a beacon of trust and innovation in the financial lending industry, continually adapting to the needs of our clients and the dynamics of the marketplace. We strive to pave the way for a future where financial support is immediate, straightforward, and tailored to each client's unique circumstances.

Our Story

CapitalTrust Loans was founded on the principle that financial assistance should be readily available to those who need it, without the complexity and delays often associated with traditional lending. Our founders, seasoned in the financial sector, noticed a gap in the market for swift, uncomplicated loan services. They envisioned a company that would not only lend money but also extend a hand to guide clients towards financial stability and success.

Our Loan Products

At CapitalTrust Loans, we understand that financial needs are as diverse as the clients we serve. That's why we offer a variety of loan products designed to meet you where you are — and take you where you want to go. Explore our range of financial solutions:

Business Growth Loan

Product Code: BGL-2023

Tailored for small to medium-sized businesses aiming to expand operations, purchase new equipment, or invest in research and development. Offers variable interest rates with medium-term repayment plans. Requires a detailed business plan and financial statements for application..

Home Advantage Loan

Product Code: HAL-2023

A long-term mortgage product designed for individuals looking to purchase or refinance their homes. Offers competitive fixed or variable interest rates, with loan terms up to 30 years. Features flexible repayment options and is ideal for first-time homebuyers or those seeking to invest in real estate.

Education First Loan

Product Code: EFL-2023

A loan product designed for students or professionals seeking financial assistance for higher education or specialized training. Offers lower interest rates and a grace period until completion of studies, followed by a flexible repayment schedule.

Quick Cash Personal Loan

Product Code: QCPL-2023

A short-term loan solution for individuals needing immediate cash for emergencies or unexpected expenses. Features a simple application process, rapid approval, and competitive interest rates. Repayment terms are short, catering to those who need quick financial support without long-term commitments.

Vehicle Financing Loan

Product Code: VFL-2023

Designed for individuals or businesses looking to purchase new or used vehicles. Offers competitive rates and flexible repayment terms, with the vehicle serving as collateral for the loan.

Holiday Dream Loan

Product Code: HDL-2023

A specialized loan product designed for individuals or families planning for a dream vacation or a significant holiday trip. This loan offers flexible amounts sufficient to cover travel, accommodation, and related expenses. It features competitive interest rates and a convenient repayment plan that allows borrowers to repay the loan after their holiday, with terms ranging from 6 to 18 months. The application process is streamlined for quick approval, ensuring timely access to funds for holiday planning. This product is perfect for those who wish to make special memories without the immediate financial strain.
